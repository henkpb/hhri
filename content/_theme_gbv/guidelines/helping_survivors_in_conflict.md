This training manual has been developed for helpers who provide assistance and support to women who survive gender-based violence (GBV) and sexual trauma during disasters, conflicts and emergency situations, where access to health professionals with psychological or psychiatric expertise is limited. It is also available in [Arabic](http://www.hhri.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/hhri_web_AR_HR.pdf), [Russian](http://www.hhri.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/HHRI_RU_GBV.pdf), [Portuguese](https://viewer.joomag.com/portugese-mental-health-and-gender-based-violene-2019/0086832001602231052?short&), [Nepali](https://www.hhri.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/hhri_NP_web.pdf), [Ukrainian](https://viewer.joomag.com/ukrainian-mental-health-and-gender-based-violene-2016/0178566001618434528?short&), [Norwegian](https://www.hhri.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/hhri_2020_woman_norsk_p1.pdf), [Mongolian](https://www.hhri.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/03/Mogolsk-Manual.pdf) and [Spanish](http://www.hhri.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/hhri_es_v2.pdf) versions. We will also like to introduce you to our [Gender based violence manual – website](http://www.hhri.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/hhri_es_v2.pdf).
