Dear colleague, welcome to our Gender Based Violence Training Manual page.

We hope that this low-threshold manual will be useful in your work and that you will benefit from the knowledge presented in it. For easy access to information about the content of the manual please watch our [tutorials](https://www.hhri.org/women-manual/resources/tutorials/).

Here you can access to various [lectures](https://www.hhri.org/lectures/) on topics related to gender-based violence and our manuals.

You can find the manual in each of these languages: [English](https://viewer.joomag.com/mental-health-and-gender-based-violence-2016/0467236001618345303?short&), [Spanish](https://viewer.joomag.com/spanish-mental-health-and-gender-based-violence/0336059001618385074?short&), [Russian](https://viewer.joomag.com/russian-mental-health-and-gender-based-violence-russian-version/0588272001504616112?short&), [Arabic](https://viewer.joomag.com/arabic-mental-health-and-gender-based-violence-2016/0178758001618347203?short&), [Portuguese](https://viewer.joomag.com/portugese-mental-health-and-gender-based-violene-2019/0086832001602231052?short&), [Nepalese](https://www.hhri.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/hhri_NP_web.pdf), [Ukrainian](https://viewer.joomag.com/ukrainian-mental-health-and-gender-based-violene-2016/0178566001618434528?short&), [Norwegian](https://www.hhri.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/hhri_2020_women_NO_web-2024.pdf) and [Mongolian](https://www.hhri.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/03/Mogolsk-Manual.pdf).

In each of the modules, you can select either the download format or the online reading format. You can also find the manual and other support elements in different languages.

Some core elements of the manual
  - A human rights-based approach
  - With a gender perspective
  - Mental health / psychological consequences of trauma
  - Resource-oriented (respect / opportunities / hope)
  - Focus on the helper
  - Metaphors, symbols, and stories
  - Psychoeducational approach
  - Own experiences and reactions / Body-oriented exercises
